“We Are Your Retribution”: Trump’s Threat to the American People

In March 2023, Donald Trump stood before a cheering crowd at CPAC and delivered a line that sent shockwaves through the democratic world: “I am your retribution.”

That statement wasn’t aimed at corrupt elites or foreign powers. It was aimed at everyday Americans—journalists, protesters, union workers, educators, immigrants, LGBTQ+ citizens—anyone who dares to disagree.

Retribution isn’t policy. It’s punishment. And when a political movement pledges revenge instead of reform, democracy is on life support.

Trump’s MAGA coalition continues to echo his authoritarian tone. Sen. J.D. Vance has questioned universal suffrage. Rep. Paul Gosar called for public executions of political opponents.

This is not rhetoric. It’s the normalization of state violence—wrapped in red, white, and blue.

Your Rights in the Crosshairs

The 1st Amendment guarantees freedom of speech and peaceful assembly. MAGA leaders routinely demonize journalists, label protesters “terrorists,” and pass laws to criminalize dissent.

The 14th Amendment ensures equal protection under the law. Trump’s calls to end birthright citizenship threaten to shred it completely.

The 4th and 5th Amendments protect against unlawful search, seizure, and prosecution. Project 2025 would grant the president unchecked power over federal law enforcement.

This isn’t hypothetical. It’s happening. In public. In plain sight.
